Favorite Websites

Facebook

I use this platform to stay updated with news and connected with my community. It is also a great tool for networking with fellow creatives. Following various multimedia pages also helps me stay informed about current trends in the Philippines.

YouTube

YouTube is my primary source for entertainment. I also often watch tech reviews to stay updated on the latest hardware that can improve my digital art workflow. The platform serves as a constant source of creative inspiration from creators around the world.

Shopee

This is my go-to destination for finding affordable tech accessories and art supplies for my personal projects. I enjoy browsing through various gadget stores to find tools that enhance my workstation. The convenience of local shipping makes it easy to upgrade my setup whenever I need new equipment.

Pinterest

I rely on Pinterest to build mood boards and gather visual references for my graphic design assignments. It is an essential tool for exploring unique color palettes and reference. Whenever I have a creative block, a quick scroll through my saved pins helps me find a fresh perspective.
